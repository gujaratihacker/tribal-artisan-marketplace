Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I contact an artisan directly?

Visit our Artisans page, find the artisan you'd like to connect with, and use the Call or Message buttons on their profile.

How can I register as an artisan?

Fill out the contact form above and select "I want to register as an artisan." Our team will reach out to help you set up your profile and list your products.

Is there any fee to join the platform?

No. Tribal Crafts is free for both artisans and buyers. We believe in removing barriers, not adding them. Our goal is to help artisans earn fair income.

Do you handle shipping and delivery?

Shipping is arranged directly between the buyer and artisan. We can help coordinate logistics for larger orders, but most transactions are handled directly between the two parties.
